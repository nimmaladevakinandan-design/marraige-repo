Haldi

14 May 2025

On the bride’s side, the atmosphere was filled with warmth, laughter, and love. Surrounded by her closest family and friends, she sat gracefully as turmeric paste was gently applied to her face, hands, and feet. The women sang traditional songs, shared stories, and blessed her with happiness and prosperity. The yellow hues of haldi symbolized purity and a fresh start as she prepared for her big day.


Meanwhile, the groom’s Haldi was equally lively. His friends and cousins made it a fun-filled event, smearing him with turmeric and even chasing him around with extra bowls of the paste! His ceremony had a more playful vibe, with water splashes, flower showers, and lots of dancing

Pelli-Koduku

Pelli-Koduku

14 May 2025

The Pellikoduku ceremony is one of the most cherished and culturally rich pre-wedding rituals in Telugu weddings. It is usually held a day or two before the wedding and is dedicated to the groom, marking the beginning of his journey into married life.


During this ceremony, the groom is treated like a prince. He is dressed in traditional attire—usually a dhoti and kurta. The ritual begins with a Ganapathi puja to seek blessings for a smooth and auspicious wedding. The elders of the family bless the groom, The event is filled with joy, music, and vibrant decorations. Friends and family gather to celebrate, take photos, and enjoy festive food. It’s not just a ritual—it’s a celebration of love, tradition, and the coming together of families..

Pelli-Kuthuru

Pelli-kuthuru

14 May 2025

Building on the joy after the Haldi, the Pelikuthuru day became a beautiful tapestry of blessings and cherished moments. Relatives, both close and distant, gathered to shower the bride-to-be with their heartfelt good wishes. There was a palpable sense of warmth and tradition in the air as elders performed rituals, applying auspicious pastes and offering prayers for her prosperous married life.


Laughter and conversation filled the home as everyone reminisced, shared stories, and celebrated this significant transition. The bride, adorned and radiant, absorbed every blessing, every loving glance, and every word of advice. It was a day where the strong bonds of family were truly evident, creating a treasure trove of happy memories for her to carry into her new journey.
